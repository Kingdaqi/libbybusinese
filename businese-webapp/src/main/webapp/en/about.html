Company Profile

Tianjin ratel international trade co., LTD., founded in 2019, is registered in huayuan industrial zone of tianjin binhai high-tech zone. Based on the company's customer positioning and operation mode, the company is mainly engaged in the import and export of self-operated and agent goods and technologies. At present, the company's business scope mainly includes quartz glass products export, electrical equipment export, quartz glass products domestic trade, etc. Since its establishment, the company has always adhered to the development strategy of "going global" and vigorously developed foreign trade on the basis of mutual benefit. At present, the company has in-depth exchanges and cooperation with Russia, the United States and other regions. The company has a long-term and stable resource channels, has cultivated a strong business ability, familiar with the international trade rules of the staff team, has a good business reputation in the industry.

Business Scope

Import and export of goods or technologies (except those prohibited by the state or involving administrative examination and approval); Electronic products, optical quartz glass products and related accessories, steel and valves, electrical equipment, mechanical equipment, toys, plastic products, lighting equipment, sports supplies, ceramic products, household appliances, solar energy equipment, building decoration materials, labor insurance supplies, arts and crafts, clothing accessories, shoes and hats, hardware tools, metal materials wholesale and retail; Information transmission, software and information technology services; Business services.
